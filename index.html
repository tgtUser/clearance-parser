Clearance Workload Parser

Instructions

  • Log in at greenfield.target.com
  • Click on Dashboards in the menu
  • Search for Price Change Forecast in the search bar
  • In the Filter By menu, check the store number and the week you want to parse, then click Apply
  • Click on the Price Change Forecast Card itself
  • Export the card to CSV, if given an option Download the file - do not open it in Excel
  • Click the Choose File button below, then find the downloaded file.
  • Click the Submit button below
